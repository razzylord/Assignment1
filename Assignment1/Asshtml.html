Spaghetti Carbonara

This classic Italian pasta dish is creamy, comforting, and surprisingly quick to make. Using just a few ingredients, you can create a restaurant-quality meal at home in less than 30 minutes. Perfect for weeknight dinners or special occasions.

Recipe Information

Preparation Time: 10 minutes

Cooking Time: 15 minutes

Servings: 4

Difficulty Level: Beginner

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta or bacon, diced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook spaghetti until al dente.
  2. In a skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add pancetta and cook until crispy.
  3. Add minced garlic to the skillet and sauté for 1 minute.
  4. In a bowl, whisk eggs and Parmesan cheese together until smooth.
  5. Drain spaghetti (reserve ½ cup pasta water) and add to skillet. Remove from heat.
  6. Quickly stir in the egg-cheese mixture, adding reserved pasta water if needed for creaminess.
  7. Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper. Serve immediately.

Tips

For an extra flavor boost, use freshly grated Parmesan and freshly cracked pepper. Avoid scrambling the eggs by ensuring the skillet is off the heat before mixing.
